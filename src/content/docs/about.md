*The Technical Writer's Guide to AI* is intended to be a living, collaborative guide to AI for technical writers.
It is just getting started.

## Purpose

This guide was started because there are many technical writers who can benefit from guidance regarding AI.
We could also use a durable place to record questions of the moment, and share how people have actually answered them.

## Who it's for

There are at least three audiences:

1. Newcomers: technical writers who are new to AI and do not know where to start.
2. Ambivalent professionals: technical writers who are uneasy about AI, but who want to understand it rather than ignore it.
3. Practitioners: people who are already using AI in documentation, and who want to compare their approach with others'.

This guide assumes that you, the reader, can read a diff, and that you care about whoever is on the other end of the page.
It does not assume you have decided anything, and treats ambivalence as reasonable.
